HOW TO PLAY

  • 1Tap to launch the ball toward the hoop.
  • 2Make a basket to climb higher as the court scrolls up.
  • 3Hoops alternate between the left and right corners.
  • 4Tap again mid-air to steer toward the next rim.
  • 5Leave the top or bottom of the screen and it's game over.
  • 6Chain clean swishes for combo bonuses and a hot streak.
